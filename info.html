About Twineshots

Your story, beautifully told

Who We Are

Twineshots is a Kampala-based photography and videography company established to capture life's most important moments. From intimate celebrations to large-scale corporate events, we bring a creative eye and professional precision to every project we take on.

Our professional team combines years of hands-on experience with state-of-the-art equipment to deliver visual content that exceeds expectations. We believe that every story deserves to be told with authenticity, artistry, and attention to detail.

Whether it's a wedding, a corporate brand shoot, real estate photography, or a live-streamed event, Twineshots is committed to turning your vision into a lasting visual legacy. Our creative approach ensures that no two projects look alike -- because your story is uniquely yours.

Mission & Vision

What drives us forward every day

Our Mission

To capture and preserve life's most meaningful moments through exceptional photography and videography. We are dedicated to delivering creative, high-quality visual content that tells compelling stories and creates lasting memories for every client we serve.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted creative studio, recognized for our commitment to innovation, artistic excellence, and client satisfaction. We aspire to set the standard for visual storytelling across the region and beyond, one frame at a time.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped who we are

2016

Founded in Kampala

Twineshots was born from a passion for visual storytelling. We started with a small team and a big dream to capture moments that matter.

2018

Opened Main Studio

We opened our professional studio at Elements Courts along Makerere Kasubi road, providing a dedicated space for shoots, editing, and client consultations.

2020

Launched Live Streaming Services

Adapting to a changing world, we introduced professional live streaming capabilities, enabling clients to broadcast their events to audiences worldwide.

2023

Expanded to Corporate & Real Estate

We broadened our portfolio to include corporate brand photography, architectural shoots, and real estate videography, serving a growing roster of business clients.
